RETURN POLICY

TAC allows Customers to make returns for goods purchased on its platform in accordance with the terms and conditions below.

Whilst TAC would want to extend this right of return to all goods sold on its platform, some Goods are however exempted from returns. Please see Eligibility clause, number 2 for goods exempted for return.

To qualify for returns, you can only return an item within 2 days from the date of delivery.

Eligibility:

  1. You may initiate a return within 2 days from the date of delivery If you receive the wrong item or if the item is damaged or not functional, or a counterfeit.

  1. You may also reject an Item at the point of delivery or return an item if you change your mind. However, the following items are exempted from returns in this case: Pastries, Customized products, underwear, perfumes, cosmetics, hair extensions and jewelry.

  1. To be eligible for returns, all items must be returned in their original packaging with manuals intact and any tags or seals unbroken. Any accessories or gifts attached to items packages must be returned alongside the items, otherwise, your return will be invalid.

Instructions for Return:

  1. To Initiate a return, please send a Request to tacavid@gmail.com or contact a member of our Customer Service Team. You must include pictures of the item delivered to you, as well as the Order number.
  2. Return Authorization: Once your claim has been validated, we will schedule a pickup date with you.
  3. Place the item in its original packaging, with any accessories, tags, labels or freebies intact. Always ensure your Return Slip is signed by our agents as this will serve as a proof of your return.
  4. Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and where eligible, we will process a refund to the purchaser debit or credit card used for the transaction.
  5. Refunds will be completed between 2-3 working days.
  6. If your item is not eligible for return/refunds, the item will be re-delivered to you.

Cancellation Policy

  • All Orders can be canceled within 24hrs of Order placement.
  • Express Orders can only be cancelled within 1 Hour of Order placement.
  • 100% refund for your item plus delivery fee if canceled within 24hrs of order placement.
  • An order cannot be canceled once it has been shipped. If you decide to cancel after shipment, our standard return policy applies.

Return policy for Perishable Items
