Our Story

Our Founder, Abiodun Jagun being the only one out of his siblings living in Nigeria with the rest of his family in Diaspora happened to be the only link to connect his siblings abroad whenever they needed to express love and send gift items to their mother.


Irrespective of their geographical differences they were at peace that their mother was well taken care of. He realized the value of him being present as it would have been quite worrisome if there wasn’t a reliable person physically present to deliver gifts to his mom especially at special occasions and also take care of her. He also got requests from lots of his friends and friends of his siblings to reach out to their loved ones back home in Nigeria. All of these led him to identify a huge gap that needed to be filled for Nigerians who live out of the country in the United States of America, Europe, Canada, Australia e.tc and have no reliable and trusted means of reaching out to their loved ones in cash, gifts and in kind.


TAC was conceived as a means to fill in this gap where Nigerians in Diaspora can easily reach out to their loved ones’ back home in form of gifts and other services including but not limited to healthcare, money remittance, property purchase etc. by providing transparent and trusted concierge services to its customers at their utmost comfort through an online platform.
